Everyday Kazakh қой/ғой The particle ғой is used very often in colloquial speech. It does not have a separate word. It functions to express the context of a sentence. It is used in the following situations: 1. It is used as a polite word instead of please, especially for young kids. For example, Балам, дүкенге барып қайта ғой.=Son, can you go to the store, please. Бүгін сабақта не істедің? Айта ғой.=What did you do today at school? Tell me, please. Балалар, жата ғойыңдар енді, кеш болып кетті.=Kiddos, go to bed please. It is too late now. 2. In interrogative sentences it can be translated as a tag sentence For example, Ceн өткенде мына телефонды осы жерден алдың ғой?=You bought this phone from here, didn’t you? Мама, ертең бәріміз бірге паркке барамыз ғой?=Mummy, we are going to park together tomorrow, aren’t we? Біз қазір Айнұрдың үйіне бара жатырмыз ғой?=We are going to Ainur’s house right now, aren’t we? 3. It may express a feeling of being right. For example, Мен айттым ғой!=I told you! (You see, I was right.) Білдім ғой осылай боларын!=I knew it! (I had this feeling. But you did not listen to me. I was right.) 4. It may be used as saying didn’t you know that. For example, Олар кеше келді ғой.=They arrived yesterday. (Didn’t you know about that. I thought you knew.) Ол менің ағам ғой.=He is my brother. (Didn’t you know about that. I thought you knew.) 5. It is used to highlight the verb in a sentence. For example, Сені сағындым ғой! = I miss you so much! Білсең ғой қалай қиналғанымды!=If you only knew how much I suffered! 6. ғой expresses surprise or the moment when you realise something. For example, Бүгін мейрам екен ғой! = Oh, I totally forgot, it is a holiday today. Ahh. Ооо! Мынау сенің үлкен қызың екен ғой! Өзіңнен аумай қалыпты! = Oh, this is your eldest daughter!
