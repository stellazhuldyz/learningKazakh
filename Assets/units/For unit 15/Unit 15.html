Қазақ тілі және өркениет Grammar Көмекші етістіктер-Auxiliary verbs In Kazakh language sometimes some contextual information about the situation might be expressed with the help of auxiliary verbs. These verbs can function either as individual full verbs with its own meaning or they may go in pairs with the main verb to give additional information to the situation. For instance, the verb беру may function as to give as the main verb, but as an auxiliary verb in pair with another verb it means to start doing something. For example: Full verb: ● Кітапхана кітапты уақытша қолдануға береді.=Library gives a book for temporary use. Auxiliary verb: ● -Баланы киіндіре берейін бе?-Should I start dressing up the kid? ● -Иә, киіндіре бер. - Yes, start dressing him up. The following table includes the most widely used auxiliary verbs and their meanings in pair with another verbs: № Main verb Auxiliary verb 1 Тұру = to stay/to live. Мен Алматыда тұрамын = I live in Almaty. ып/іп/п тұру = to do something time by time/sometimes Арасында қалаға барып тұрамын. = Time by time I go to the city. а/е/й тұру = to do something for a short period of time Мен келгенше балаға қарай тұр. = Take care of a kid while I am outside (literally before I arrive back). This implies that she will be out for a very short time. Маған қарызға 10 000 теңге бере тұрасың ба? = Would you kindly lend me 10 000 tenge. This implies that she will give it back in a short period of time. 2 Қалу = to stay/to remain Олар Алматыда қалды. = They remained in Almaty. ып/іп/п қалу = to do something accidentally/unexpectedly/not on purpose. It implies that you are sorry about that. ● Сабаққа кешігіп қалдық. = We were late for the class. (Not on purpose. We did not expect it, but unfortunately it happened). ● Кеше жолда құлап қалдым. = I fell down yesterday in the street. 3 ып/іп/п қала жаздау=almost do something, which should not be done or happen. Құлап қала жаздадым.=I almost fell down. (But I did not, luckily.). 4 Қою = to put Ол кітапты үстелдің (столдың) үстіне қойды. = He puts the the book on the table. ып/іп/п қою = to do something accidentally/unexpectedly/not on purpose. It implies that you are sorry about that. Depending on the main verb either қалу or қою can be used for the same meaning. Байқамай, оған бәрін айтып қойдым. = I accidentally told him everything. (This was something that was not meant to be told. It should have been a secret.). 5 ып/іп/п қоя жаздау = almost do something, which should not be done or happen. Байқамай, оған бәрін айтып қоя жаздадым. = I almost told him everything. ((But I did not, luckily.). 6. Кету = to leave Ол Алматыдан кеше кетті. = He left Almaty yesterday. ып/іп/п кету = when something happens suddenly and unexpectedly; when someone is taken by surprise. This construction can sometimes be used to express excuse for your actions Қуанып кету; түсіп кету; шаршап кету; алдап кету; ұйықтап кету. Жиналыста отырып, ұйықтап кетіппін. = I fell asleep during the meeting. (I should not have to, but it was unintentional). 6 Шығу = to exit/to go out/to leave Балалар кеште мектептен шығады. = Children will leave the school in the evening. ып/іп/п шығу = to finish something at its fullest, totally. Мариям үйдің бәрін жинап шықты. = Maryiam cleaned the whole house fully. Compare with Мариям үйдің бәрін жинады. = Maryiam cleaned the house. In the first example it is highlighting the fact that this action has fully been finished. шығу – результат Мен үйдің бәрін жинап шықтым. Олар үлкен жоба жасап шықты. 7 Беру = to give Біз ертең концерт береміз = We are going to give a concert tomorrow. а/е/й беру = to do something continuously, again and again. Ол бір өлеңді қайта-қайта айта берді.= She kept singing the same song again and again. 8 а/е/й беру = to start doing something. This construction is usually used in conversations to ask for permission to start doing something. Сабақ айта берсем бола ма? = May I start answering the class task? Иә, болады. Бастай бер. = Yes, you may, Start answering. 9 Салу = to put into Мен қазанға ет салдым. = I have put the meat into a cauldron. а/е/й салу = to do something with ease, not making much effort on that so I can do this favour for you easily. Мен есікті жаба саламын. = I will close the door. (Do not worry about this. I will do it for you.) 10 Жіберу = to send Балаларды мектепке жібердім. = I have sent the kids to the school. ып/іп/п жіберу = do a favour for someone Балаларға көмектесіп жібердік. = We helped the kids. (We did it as a favour. It was not difficult for us). 11 Болу = to be/to become Үлкейгенде ғарышкер боламын. = I am going to become a cosmonaut when I grew up. ып/іп/п болу = to finish Сабағымды оқып болдым. = I have finished doing my home assignments. 12 Алу = to take/to receive/to get Олар сабақтан бес алды. = They got A marks in their classes. ып/іп/п алу = to finish something (successfully) before starting another action. Сен келмей тұрып, үйдің бәрін жинап алдым. = Before your arrival, I tidied up the whole house. (Literally: Until you did not arrive, I tidied up the whole house.). 13. Көру = to see/to watch Кеше бәріміз қызық кино көрдік. = Yesterday we all watched a very interesting movie. ып/іп/п көру = to try something, to make an effort. Мен жазып көрейінші. = May I try to write it down, please. 14. Тастау = to throw away/to get rid of something Ол артық заттардың бәрін далаға тастады. = He threw away/get rid of all extra things. ып/іп/п тастау = to do something with ease and delightfully, enjoying it. Ойымдағының бәрін айтып-айтып тастадым. = I told every single thing I wanted to say (and I am so happy that I could do it and feel so proud and relieved for doing so). 15. Түсу = to descend/to go down/get off/get out of Ол аттан түсті. = He get off the horse. а/е/й түсу = to keep doing something more intensively Күн төбеге көтерілген сайын, шақырая түсті. = As the sun was rising above it was getting hotter. Vocabulary Phrases with the word көңіл Көңіл words: The word көңіл (mood) has very important meaning in Kazakh culture. Most words in English, which express emotions, do not have an exact translation in Kazakh. This is related to cultural differences in approaching and expressing emotions. Kazakh culture is usually more moderate in expressing emotions. Being reserved implies good upbringing and culturally correct behaviour. Many words to describe emotions and mood are expressed with the phrases with көңіл. Егер is translated as if. A verb in the dependent clause with if always takes ending са/се. The word егер can be omitted, but ending са/се implies the same meaning. For example: Ертең қолым тисе, қалаға барып қайтамын.=If I have time tomorrow, I will go to the city.
