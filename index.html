The training manual is designed for learners of Kazakh as foreign language. It consists of 15 units and can be used at universities, colleges, private courses, or for individual study, both individually and in groups. The training manual aims to fill a void for learners of Kazakh in English. Each unit includes sections on grammar, reading, vocabulary, speaking, literary analysis, everyday Kazakh usage, and writing. Transcripts to videos, answer keys, vocabulary list for each unit and additional grammar sources are provided at the end of the textbook. Students can access additional resources on the following web-page: www.learn-kazakh.com.
